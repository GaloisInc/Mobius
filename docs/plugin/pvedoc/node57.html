Downloading

Figure 3.1: The Mobius PVE Download Site at KindSoftware.
The Mobius PVE is available from the MOBIUS Trac server http://mobius.ucd.ie/ and via the KindSoftware research group website (the research group of the MOBIUS UCD partner), as seen in Fig. 3.1. Pre-configured Eclipse builds are available for all main platforms including Microsoft Windows, Apple's Mac OS X, and Linux. Download the appropriate archive; it can be unpacked anywhere on the local disk.
